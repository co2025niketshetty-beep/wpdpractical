Indian National Flag

Overview

The National Flag of India represents the unity, integrity, and sovereignty of the nation.

Meaning of Colors

Saffron: Courage and sacrifice

White: Peace and truth

Green: Growth and prosperity

Ashoka Chakra

The Ashoka Chakra represents law and movement, with 24 spokes.

Interesting Facts

  • Adopted on 22 July 1947
  • Designed by Pingali Venkayya
  • Ratio is 2:3
